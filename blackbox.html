Black Box Investigation

By Esmatullah Wahidi 3A

Claim

In our tech class at ESUMS, we were told to make a hypothesis about what was inside the blackbox, we first off made a picture of our hypothesis, after we made the inside of the mystery box, what I think inside the black box is multiple tubes,funnels, to transport the water through the blackbox to the bottom.

Evidence

The blackbox is a box that Mr.Sinusas made for us to think about what's inside. I made a visual of it in my head and tried different parts to see if they would work like what Mr.Sinusas told us. The results were very consistent and always were near the same numbers as the amount of water we had put inside, We used 1000 ML of water (input) and the output would be like 800-900 ML (output) sometimes it was 1000 ML of water that came ßout, For ex. In test 4 1000 ML were put in and 900 came out of the box, in test 35 1000 ML of water was put in and 800 came out of the box, For how we tested we would put in a certain amount of water so we can calculate the difference between how much water came out and how much we put in.

Photography

We were also given a camera to take pictures of when we were doing the project. I took multiple pictures while doing the project and showed the work during the making of the model and making the model in Maya. We also took pictures while testing the model, collecting data to see if our claim would be correct or how we could change any errors in the model.

We made pictures of our models first to show how we would make our model. I made my pictures in photoshop, after we finished doing that we started to go into animating the pictures. We animated the water flow through our box and how we wanted the model to work. We also used multiple types of brushes to show the different types of material we will be using and color.

We also made a model in Maya to show how it would look in real life and make a flowing animation in Maya. We learned various skills in Maya and how to make animations and how to rig human parts and tubes.
